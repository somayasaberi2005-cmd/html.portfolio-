Afghanistan, known as the heart of Asia, has a history as old as time itself.
For thousands of years, it stood at the crossroads of great empires — where
the Silk Road carried traders, scholars, and dreamers between East and West.
From the mighty empires of Alexander the Great and Genghis Khan to the birth
of powerful dynasties like the Durrani Empire, Afghanistan has always been a
land of resilience and pride. Its mountains have witnessed countless battles,
yet its spirit remains unbroken — a land rich in culture, poetry, and courage
that continues to inspirethe world.
