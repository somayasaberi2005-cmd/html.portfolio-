Kabuli Pulao (Afghan Rice with Meat, Carrots & Raisins)
🧂 Ingredients:
2 cups basmati rice
500 g lamb or beef, cut into chunks
2 carrots, cut into thin strips
½ cup raisins
1 onion, chopped
3 tablespoons oil or ghee
½ teaspoon cumin powder
½ teaspoon cardamom powder
½ teaspoon cinnamon powder
Salt and black pepper (to taste)
3 cups water or broth
A handful of almonds or pistachios (optional, for garnish)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:


Cook the meat:
In a pot, heat oil and fry onions until golden.
Add meat, salt, and pepper. Fry until brown.
Pour in water or broth and cook until tender (about 45 minutes).
Prepare rice:
Wash and soak the basmati rice for 20–30 minutes, then drain.
Cook carrots and raisins:
In a separate pan, fry carrots in a bit of oil until soft.
Add raisins and fry for 1–2 minutes. Remove and set aside.
Combine rice and meat:
When meat is ready, remove it and keep the broth.
Add rice to the broth and cook until the water is almost absorbed.
Layer and steam:
Layer meat on top of the rice, then add fried carrots and raisins.
Sprinkle cumin, cardamom, and cinnamon on top.
Cover tightly and steam on low heat for 20 minutes.
Serve:
Gently mix before serving so rice doesn’t break.
Garnish with nuts if you like. Serve hot and enjoy! 🧡
